Sea Song Bathhouse

Please be aware that Sea Song Bathhouse is an 18+ RP environment. Please respect fellow guests and staff. Please read establishment rules below fully.

Welcome to the Sea Song Bathhouse, a quiet retreat.

Location: Crystal DC, Mateus, Shirogane Ward 23, Sub Apt 70

Hours: The bathhouse is open for visitor use at all times. Staff will be present during some evening hours when announced by Party Finder

Cost: There is no fee to use the bathhouse. Tips are welcome and are put towards decorations and eventual relocation to a house if possible.

Features: The establishment features three baths as well as a modest selection of drinks. All drinks are served via RP and no money or items are exchanged.

Haerbryda Sykurwyn, manager

HOUSE RULES

1. Seas Song Bathhouse is an inclusive environment, both IC and OOC. Homophobic, transphobic, racially insenstive behavior are grounds for immediate removal and ban.

2. While we welcome RP of all kinds, we request ERP be kept to party and DM chat for the comfort of our other guests.

3. The bathhouse is open players 18 years of age and older. Characters, likewise, must be at least 18 years of age to use the facilities.

4. Use of the bathhouse is not implicit consent for ERP and we ask guests to maturely approach such topics and respect others' boundaries.

5. Rudeness, disruptive behavior or hindering other people's enjoyment of the space will not be tolerated. Please refrain from the use of spells in the space.

6. Weapons, pets and minions are not allowed in the bathhouse. If your character requires an exception to this for any reason such as a guide animal, do not hesitate to inform staff.

7. Do not do something that warrants adding a rule 7.
